##Chockolate fondant recipe

##Preparations

-melt half of chocolate on low fire

##Ingredients

-Dark chocolate: 100g
-Wheat flour: 300g
-Eggs: 1 medium 
-Butter: 50g

##Preparation

-Mix butter and melted chocolate
-Slowly add and mix egg into butter-chocolate mix
-Add flour and mix everything thoroughly
-Add dough into muffing molds and insert piece of chocolate in the middle of your cake
-Bake at temperature 200 degrees C for 10 minutes
